# **App Name**: Oktano

## Core Features:

- Immutable Cashback Ledger: A high-integrity double-entry bookkeeping engine that manages every credit and debit of loyalty points, ensuring total auditability and preventing concurrency errors during heavy transaction periods.
- Predictive Behavioral Tool: An AI-powered mission engine that utilizes a tool to generate personalized fueling goals based on driving habits, applying 'loss aversion' and 'endowed progress' to optimize user retention.
- Resilient Offline Passport: An offline-first architecture for the instant generation and display of QR codes for pump activation and discount vouchers, designed to function perfectly in remote gas station zones.
- Smart-Path Navigator: Utilizes advanced geospatial calculations to suggest the most cost-efficient refueling stops based on the actual driving route rather than simple proximity, optimizing true economic savings.
- Telemetry Shield: Strict security protocol that validates GPS telemetry in real-time to detect and prevent spoofed locations during discount redemption at the pump.
- Fleet Ops Dashboard: A unified administrative console for station owners to monitor cashback circulation, manage dynamic mission rules, and view real-time performance analytics.
- Tiered Membership Nexus: Management of premium subscription levels with unique benefits, built to eventually integrate with vehicle telemetry sensors for deep consumption monitoring.

## Style Guidelines:

- The palette is centered on the concept of 'Machined Precision'. Primary color: Deep Indigo-Violet (#7A5CFF) symbolizing high-performance digital power.
- Background color: Obsidian Dark Slate (#121017) to provide elite contrast for high-stakes financial data and easy readability at nighttime refueling.
- Accent color: Cobalt Cyan (#6BB3FF) for interactive elements, providing a bright, technical contrast against the darker theme.
- A pairing of 'Space Grotesk' for tech-oriented headlines and 'Inter' for all body text to maintain maximum legibility and an objective, machined look.
- Utilize minimalist, monolinear stroke icons that evoke professional diagnostic tools and automotive engineering charts.
- High-velocity checkout interface with a focused QR 'Quick-Action' button prioritized at the thumb's reach for maximum operational speed.
- Subtle atomic pulses on successful syncing events and linear progression fills for mission trackers to reward cognitive progress.
